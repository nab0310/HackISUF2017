About the Isolation Cube

The Isolation Cube is here as a test of society. The person in the cube is bound to the will of his observers, directing his life from far away. What happens in the isolation cube is a reflection of society. Whether our participant leaves sane or as a husk of his former self is all on us.


To participate in the game of Isolation Cube, you can watch a live stream from the isolation cube, showing the participant in real time. Then to control his life and environment you can send DM's from your twitter account to @projectcube1 to vote and submit actions. Check out the commands tab to see how to do that. In general you can command the person in the room to do anything (that doesn't break the basic rules at the bottom) and you can also command that something be done to the participant or the environment by a "conductor" who only enters the room to perform those actions. Every 15 minutes the action leaderboard is refreshed, allowing a new action to rise to the top. Whatever the the top action is at the end of a refresh period will be what happens.

Rules

  1. No open flames
  2. No complete nudity
  3. No significant injuries
  4. Nothing illegal
  5. No animals
  6. No damage to the room
