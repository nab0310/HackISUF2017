About the Isolation Cube

The Isolation Cube is a HackISU project created by 3 developers in the Fall of 2017. The core concept is to have a participant in the 'Isolation Cube' and give twitter users the ability to send and vote on different requests for participant to complete.

Users are able to tweet their suggested requests at our twitter account [link] in hopes of it being a choosen suggestion. When a suggestion is tweeted by a user it is given an id that can then be used for voting. Every [timeframe] the suggestion with the most votes is carried out by the participant. We have a list of rules in place to keep our participate safe, but outside of those the possibilites are open to the users.

Rules

  1. No open flames
  2. No lasting injuries caused to the participant
  3. Nothing illegal
  4. No animals will be harmed in the life of this project
  5. No damage to the room
  6. No nudity
